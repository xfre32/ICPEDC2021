International Conference on POWER AND EMBEDDED DRIVE CONTROL - 2021

Scope of the Conference

The 3rd International Conference on Power and Embedded Drive Control (ICPEDC - 2021) is the premier interdiscplinary platform for all researchers, professionals and engineeers from all over the world. The main objectives of ICPEDC - 2021 are to discuss the latest developments and research results in all aspects of the design, modelling, application of devices, circuits and systems related to power and embedded drive control. The conference brings together the users and researchers of electrical engineering with the emphasis of the technical content of the papers. We sincerely hope that ICPEDC - 2021 serves as a global paltform for researchers, widen professional contacts and create new oppurtunities, including establishing new collaborations.
